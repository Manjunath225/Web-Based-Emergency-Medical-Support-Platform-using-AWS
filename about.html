ABOUT MEDIRUSH

Built by a team that believes no one should suffer from a delayed medical response. We put certified doctors at your door in under 15 minutes — always.

Our Mission

EMERGENCY CARE WITH ZERO DELAY

MediRush was founded with a single, urgent idea: medical emergencies should never be delayed by logistics.

Every year, thousands of lives are lost not because care was unavailable — but because it arrived too late. Traffic, confusion, unavailable ambulances — these are solvable problems. We solved them.

Using a network of on-call doctors and paramedics strategically positioned across the city, we guarantee a certified medical professional at your location within 10–15 minutes of your request — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our platform is built for the moments that matter most. Whether it's a fever at 3am, a heart attack, or a sudden allergic reaction — MediRush is always one click away.
